About Us

Welcome to Our Motorcycle Garage!
At Our Motorcycle Garage, we're more than just a repair shop - we're a community of passionate motorcycle enthusiasts who share a deep love for motorcycles. Located in the heart of Surabaya, our garage has been serving the local motorcycle community for over 5 years, providing top-notch motorcycle services and fostering a sense of camaraderie among fellow riders.

Our mission is simple: to provide exceptional service and expertise for all your motorcycle needs. From routine maintenance to custom modifications, our team of experienced technicians is dedicated to keeping your motorcycles running smoothly and safely on the road. We believe in delivering the highest level of customer satisfaction through our commitment to quality workmanship, attention to detail, and friendly, personalized service.

Whether you need a routine maintenance service or have a custom modification project, Our Motorcycle Garage is here to help. Feel free to contact us at
